Pick Up / Deliver Instructions

  1. Tap Pick Up / Deliver.
  2. Enter Name, Company / Garage, and Build.
  3. Scan the reel label (or type the reel name in the manual input field and tap Add).
  4. Confirm the reel name appears correctly in the session list.
  5. Repeat for additional reels.
  6. When finished, export your results.
Exporting the file:
  • Tap Done (Export) to generate the file.
  • Sharing the file:
    • If the Share sheet opens, choose Mail, Files, or another option as instructed.
    • If a preview or download screen opens instead, tap More… to share or attach the file.

Return Instructions

  1. Tap Return.
  2. Enter Name, Company / Garage, and Location.
  3. Enter Reel Name, Inside Footage, and Outside Footage.
  4. Tap Add.
  5. Repeat for additional reels.
  6. Review the Return Summary.
  7. When finished, export your results.
Exporting the file:
  • Tap Done (Export) to generate the file.
  • Sharing the file:
    • If the Share sheet opens, choose Mail, Files, or another option as instructed.
    • If a preview or download screen opens instead, tap More… to share or attach the file.
